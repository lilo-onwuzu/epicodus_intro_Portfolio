About Me

Ililochi Lilo Onwuzu is an aspiring Software Engineer. She is enthused with the idea of planning, designing and developing software products where she can utilize her creativity. She received a BSc in Physics from Adelphi University in 2012 and an MSc in Applied Physics from the University of Oregon in 2013. Born in Nigeria, she now resides in Portland,Oregon. In her free time, Lilo enjoys coding, playing and listening to music, writing and exploring the outdoors.
