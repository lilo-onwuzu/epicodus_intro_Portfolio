About Me

She received a BSc in Physics from Adelphi University in 2012 and an MSc in Applied Physics from the University of Oregon in 2013. Born in Nigeria, she now resides in Portland,Oregon. In her free time, Lilo enjoys coding, playing and listening to music, writing and exploring the outdoors.
